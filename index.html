Privacy Policy

DebtFree Dashboard Content Pipeline · Last updated: July 17, 2026

The DebtFree Dashboard Content Pipeline ("the App") is a private, single-operator tool that publishes short demonstration videos of the DebtFree Dashboard product to the operator's own TikTok and Instagram accounts. It is operated by Kevin Sawler ("we", "us").

What the App does

The App automatically generates short screen-recorded product demonstration videos and posts them to social media accounts owned and controlled by the operator. It does not offer services to the public, does not have end users other than the operator, and does not post on behalf of anyone else.

Information we collect

The App collects and stores only the credentials required to publish to the operator's own accounts: OAuth access and refresh tokens issued by TikTok and Meta (Instagram), and basic profile information of the operator's own accounts (username, avatar, account ID) as returned by the TikTok Login Kit and Instagram API. No information about any other person is collected, and the App has no mechanism to do so.

How TikTok and Instagram data is used and stored

TikTok and Instagram data (OAuth tokens and the operator's own basic profile information) is used solely to authenticate the App and display the posting account's identity before publishing, as required by platform policies. Tokens are stored as encrypted secrets in a private GitHub repository (GitHub Actions encrypted secrets) and are never written to logs, source code, or any public location. Profile information is retrieved at posting time and is not retained.

What we do not do

We do not sell, share, rent, or disclose any data to third parties. We do not use data for advertising or profiling. We do not collect data from viewers of the published videos. We do not track, message, or interact with any TikTok or Instagram user through the App.

Data retention and deletion

OAuth tokens are retained only while the App is in use and can be revoked at any time from the TikTok and Instagram account security settings, or by deleting the stored secrets. Revoking access immediately disables the App's ability to post. To request deletion of any data, contact us at the address below; because the App stores only the operator's own tokens, deletion is immediate and complete.

Third-party services

The App relies on: GitHub (build automation and encrypted secret storage), ElevenLabs (voiceover generation from operator-written scripts; no personal data of others is submitted), TikTok Content Posting API, and Meta's Instagram API. Each service processes data under its own privacy policy.
