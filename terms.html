DebtFree Dashboard Content Pipeline · Last updated: July 17, 2026

The DebtFree Dashboard Content Pipeline ("the App") is a private, single-operator automation tool operated by Kevin Sawler. It publishes product demonstration videos to social media accounts owned by the operator.

Scope of use

The App is not offered to the public. There are no end users, subscriptions, or accounts other than the operator's own. Any use of the App is limited to the operator publishing content to accounts the operator owns and controls.

Content

All published content is created by the operator, consists of product demonstrations and the operator's own personal experience, and complies with the platform policies of TikTok and Meta. The App does not add third-party branding or watermarks to published content.

Platform compliance

Use of the TikTok Content Posting API and Meta's Instagram API is subject to those platforms' terms and developer policies, which the operator agrees to observe. Access can be revoked at any time from the connected accounts' security settings.
